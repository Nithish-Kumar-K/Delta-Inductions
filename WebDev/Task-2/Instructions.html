THE MILLENIUM FALCON

Captain Solo and his friend Chewbacca are trapped in a direlict space station. They are surrounded by innumerable obstacles and must successfully navigate their way out of this station. Worse, as the station is about to explode, they need to keep increasing their speed and evade the obstacles at the same time.

However, Captain Solo has one thing to his favour- Speed Capsules. Speed Capsules can warp reality around the space of the Millenium Falcon and can move it through the obstacle for a short distance. But, the Speed Capsules are limited and can be regenerated only after the Falcon moves through certain distance. Guide the Falcon through this satellite for the Captain.



CONTROLS:

UP ARROW: Provides an upward acceleration for the Falcon. Note that since it's acceleration and not velocity, the movement need not be instantaeous. Also as there is no friction the ship keeps moving in y direction till you manually bring it down to 0 velocity
DOWN ARROW: Provides a downward acceleration for the Falcon.
ENTER: Pause/Play the game. Press Enter to start the game at first
P: Play again
I: Instructions
Take care of collision against the walls of the station, due to lack of gravity you will rebound from the surface. The number of speed Capsules and score are displayed in the bottom of the screen.
